    Is this your first time here?

    For students, Use your Faculty ID (ex. 202017099999) in username and password fields to log into your account.

    For example:

    username: 202017099999

    password: 202017099999

    Once you logged in, you will be forced to change your password.

    After that, your enrolled courses will appear on the Dashboard and Site Home pages.


    To login later, use your Faculty ID (ex. 202017099999) in the username field.


    To reset your password, 

    1. follow the "forgotten username and password?" link,  

    2. enter your Faculty ID (ex. 202017099999) in the username field or enter your OFFICIAL email address (@cis.asu.edu.eg) in the Email address field. 

    3. An email will be sent to your OFFICIAL email address (@cis.asu.edu.eg), with instructions on how to get access again.

    For help, contact us at e-learning@cis.asu.edu.eg
